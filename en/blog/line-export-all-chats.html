How to Export All LINE Chat History to Text Files

Published: February 22, 2025 ・ Reading time: 7 min

Before switching phones or migrating your account, have you ever wanted to save your LINE chat history as text files?

LINE does have a built-in export feature, but it has one major limitation: you can only export one chat room at a time. If you have dozens or even hundreds of chat rooms, this is simply not practical.

In this article, we cover how to use LINE's standard export, and introduce a way to batch-export all your chat history to text at once.

1. Why You Should Save Chat History as Text

LINE chat history can also be saved via cloud backup (iCloud / Google Drive). However, keeping text files on hand offers significant advantages.

  • No app dependency: Readable from any PC or phone, even without LINE
  • Flexible search: Fast full-text search using any text editor or grep
  • Ideal for long-term storage: .txt files are a future-proof format
  • Compatible with other tools: Load into AI tools like NotebookLM for analysis
  • Insurance for account migration: Even if your backup gets corrupted, the text data remains

2. Method 1: LINE's Built-in Export

LINE has a built-in feature to save chat history as .txt files.

iPhone / Android

1

Open the chat room

Open the chat room you want to export.

2

Open the menu

Tap the ≡ icon in the top right > More > Export Chat History.

3

Choose a destination

Select a method that makes it easy to transfer to your PC, such as email, AirDrop, or Google Drive.

PC LINE (Windows / Mac)

1

Open the chat room

Open the target chat room in LINE for PC.

2

Select "Save Chat"

Click the ≡ icon in the top right > Save Chat, then choose a save location.

Output format: Exported in text format (.txt). Date, time, sender name, and message body are recorded, but images, videos, and stickers are not included (they appear as placeholders like "[Sticker]" or "[Photo]").

3. Limitations of the Built-in Feature

LINE's built-in export is convenient, but it has major limitations.

  • ✗ You can only export one chat room at a time
  • ✗ No batch export feature
  • ✗ If you have 100 chat rooms, you need to repeat the same steps 100 times
  • ✗ Group chats, individual chats, and official accounts all require separate operations
  • ✗ Easy to miss some chats during export

The reality: The average LINE user has 50 to 200 chat rooms. To export all chats with the built-in feature, you have no choice but to manually process each one individually.

10. FAQ

Q: Do I need to be logged in to LINE on my Mac?

Yes. ChatArchive reads LINE's database on your Mac. You need to have logged in to LINE for Mac at least once and have your chat history synced.

Q: Is my data sent to external servers?

No. ChatArchive is fully local. It performs no network communication whatsoever, and your data is never sent to the internet.

Q: Are images and stickers saved too?

Images and stickers that remain in LINE's cache can be displayed and saved. Older images whose cache has been cleared will appear as placeholders.

Q: Can I view the exported file on a different Mac?

Yes. Import the .chatarchive file into ChatArchive on another Mac, and you can browse it right away.
