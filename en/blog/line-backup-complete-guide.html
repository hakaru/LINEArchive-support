1. The Risk of Losing LINE Chat History

You might think "it won't happen to me," but the risk of losing LINE chat history is more common than you'd expect.

Phone Transfers and Device Failures

When switching from iPhone to Android (or vice versa), only the last 14 days of chat history can be transferred. If your phone gets water-damaged or stops working, any messages received after your last backup are gone forever.

Account Suspension or Hijacking

If LINE determines that your account has violated its terms of service, your account may be suspended and you'll lose access to all chat history. If your account is hijacked and the password is changed, you may not even be able to log in.

Backup Restoration Failures

Sometimes iCloud or Google Drive backups don't complete properly, or errors occur during restoration. A backup that shows "completed" may actually contain corrupted data that cannot be restored.

Important: LINE does not store your chat history on its servers. Your messages exist only on your device and in cloud backups (iCloud / Google Drive). If you lose both, recovery is impossible.

2. LINE's Standard Backup Methods and Their Limits

LINE offers several backup options, but each comes with significant constraints.

Method A: Automatic Backup to iCloud / Google Drive

From LINE's settings, you can automatically back up chat history to iCloud (iPhone) or Google Drive (Android).

  • ✓ Automatic periodic backups
  • ✓ Restores when transferring between the same OS
  • ✗ Cross-platform transfers (iPhone ↔ Android) limited to 14 days
  • ✗ Cannot browse or search backup data directly
  • ✗ Consumes cloud storage space
  • ✗ Encrypted and unreadable outside the LINE app

Method B: Text File Export

You can export conversations from each chat room as .txt files.

  • ✓ Plain text, readable anywhere
  • ✓ Suitable for long-term storage
  • ✗ Must be done manually, one chat room at a time
  • ✗ Images, stickers, and videos are not included
  • ✗ 100 chat rooms means 100 manual operations

Method C: PC LINE Export

LINE for Windows / Mac also has a "Save Chat" feature, but it requires the same one-by-one manual process.

Summary: None of LINE's built-in features support backing up all chats at once. For users with many chat rooms, manual export is simply not a practical option.

3. Why Bulk Backup Is Difficult

Why doesn't LINE offer a bulk backup feature? There are several technical reasons.

End-to-End Encryption

LINE messages are end-to-end encrypted (Letter Sealing). While this is excellent for security, it makes it extremely difficult for third-party tools to access message content.

Encrypted Local Database

LINE for Mac stores its database files in an encrypted format. Simply copying the database files won't let you read their contents. The database must be properly decrypted first.

No Public API

LINE does not provide a backup API for end users. This makes it challenging for third-party developers to offer official backup solutions.

The solution: ChatArchive overcomes these technical barriers by automatically detecting and decrypting LINE for Mac's local database. It reads all your chats safely without modifying the LINE app in any way.

7. FAQ

Q: Do I need to be logged in to LINE on my Mac?

Yes. ChatArchive reads LINE's database on your Mac. You need to have logged in to LINE for Mac at least once and have your chat history synced. LINE doesn't need to be running when you perform the backup — it just needs to have synced previously.

Q: How far back does the backup go?

ChatArchive backs up all chat history that has been synced to LINE for Mac. The exact range depends on your LINE app settings and device state, but it typically includes several years of chat history.

Q: Are images and stickers included in the backup?

Yes. Images and stickers that remain in LINE's cache are saved. Older images whose cache has been cleared will appear as placeholders. More recent messages are more likely to have their media intact.

Q: Can I view the backup on a different Mac?

Yes. Import the .chatarchive file into ChatArchive on another Mac and you can browse and search all your conversations immediately.

Q: Does it work with iPhone or Android?

ChatArchive is a macOS-only app. However, since LINE for Mac syncs your smartphone's chat history, you can effectively back up your iPhone or Android chat history through your Mac.
